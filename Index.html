Why these numbers, the liquid limits, and what to watch out for.
Why 3,800 calories
Maintenance (6ft, 162 lbs, very active)~3,100 cal
Muscle building surplus+300–500 cal
Extra burn on long cardio days+400–600 cal
Target to actually grow3,700–3,900 cal
At 3,300 you were likely in a deficit on hard training days. At 3,800 you gain 0.5–1 lb/week — slow, lean bulk.
